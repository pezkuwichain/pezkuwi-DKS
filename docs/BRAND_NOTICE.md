# About PezkuwiChain — Brand & Identity

## An Independent Kurdish Blockchain Project

## The Name: "Pezkuwi"

**"Pezkuwi"** is the **Kurmancî Kurdish** word for the **wild mountain goat** — the bezoar
wild goat (*Capra aegagrus*) native to the Zagros mountains of Kurdistan. The Soranî dialect
equivalent, **"bizinîkîwî"**, is used in internal technical naming (e.g. `Bizinikiwi`, the
core framework). Both refer to the same animal — the national totem of the Kurdish people,
symbolizing resilience, freedom, and the bond between Kurds and their mountains.

The Kurmancî compound combines **"pez"** (small livestock/caprine, from the ancient
Indo-Iranian root *pasu-*) and **"kûvî"** (wild, mountain-dwelling).

## The Logo & Colors

- **Central sun (Roj)** — the national symbol of the Kurdish people, from the Kurdistan flag.
- **Six petals** in a floral/rosette arrangement — the four regions of Kurdistan (Bakur,
  Başûr, Rojava, Rojhilat) plus the global diaspora, drawn from Kurdish carpet and textile motifs.
- **Kurdish national colors** — *kesk* (green: land), *sor* (red: sacrifice), *zer* (yellow: sun).

PezkuwiChain uses no color outside the Kurdish national palette. The red is the deep
Kurdish flag red (*sor*).

## Mission

PezkuwiChain exists to build decentralized, borderless digital infrastructure for the
Kurdish nation and for all stateless peoples and sovereign communities who share the values
of freedom, resilience, and self-determination.
